About

Pillow Talk has been built on the premise that we do not talk about sex enough in society

We’re infatuated by sex, but we’re embarrassed to talk about it. By not talking about sex we give rise to a host of problems around education and expectation. Too many people have really shitty sex lives!

Great sex doesn’t just happen, it needs to be carried out with skill and thoughtfulness. People’s tastes, preferences and values differ greatly and we hope Pillow Talk is a starting point to help bridge that sexual communication gap which many couples have.

We believe technology can help us express ourselves more easily, because it can reduce the emotional awkwardness of discussing certain topics face to face. We’re at the very beginning of our journey and we hope you get involved! If you have any feedback, thoughts or ideas please get in touch through twitter.
